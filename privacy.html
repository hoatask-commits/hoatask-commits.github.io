KcalMotion Dance Privacy Policy

Last Updated: July 2026

KcalMotion Dance ("the App") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how the application uses permissions and handles information while you use the App.

Camera Permission

The App requires access to your device camera only to perform real-time AI motion tracking, pose estimation and dance activity analysis. Camera frames are processed entirely on your device. No photos, videos or camera images are uploaded, stored or transmitted to our servers.

Workout Data

Workout history, calories burned, XP, levels, activity statistics and other workout information are stored locally on your device. This information is used only to provide application features and is not uploaded to any remote server.

Personal Information

KcalMotion Dance does not require user registration. We do not collect personal information such as:

  • Name
  • Email Address
  • Phone Number
  • Photos
  • Videos
  • Location

Advertising

The App uses Google AdMob to display advertisements. Google AdMob may collect advertising identifiers and limited device information in accordance with Google's own Privacy Policy. Please refer to Google's Privacy Policy for more information about how advertising data is handled.

Third-Party Services

This application may use the following Google services:

  • Google AdMob
  • Google Play Services

Data Security

Because workout analysis is performed locally on your device, your camera images remain on your device and are not transmitted to us.

Children's Privacy

KcalMotion Dance is not intended to knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe that a child has provided personal information, please contact us and we will take appropriate action.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Any future changes will be posted on this page.
